    TROJAN MALWARE
    B D F G I M R S T

    BACKDOOR TROJAN

    This Trojan has the ability to install a backdoor on your machine. It allows an attacker to get access to and control your machine. Your data could be accessed and stolen by a third party, or new malware could be downloaded and installed on your device.









    DISTRIBUTED DENIAL OF SERVICE (DDoS)

    DDoS attacks are carried out via this Trojan. The goal is to overwhelm a network with traffic and bring it down. This traffic is generated by your infected computer as well as others.










    DOWNLOADER TROJAN

    This Trojan is designed to attack a computer that has already been infected. It downloads and installs fresh malicious application versions.









    FAKE AV TROJAN

    This Trojan imitates antivirus software, but it wants money in exchange for detecting and removing threats, whether real or phony.









    GAME-THIEF TROJAN

    This Trojan aims to steal their login credentials. It's possible that the targets are online gamers.








    INFOSTEALER TROJAN

    The data on your compromised machine is what this Trojan is after.










    MAIL-FINDER TROJAN

    This Trojan is designed to steal the email addresses you've saved on your computer.









    RANSOM TROJAN

    This Trojan demands a ransom in exchange for repairing the harm it has caused to your machine. This can include encrypting your data or reducing the speed of your machine.










    REMOTE ACCESS TROJAN

    Through a remote network connection, this Trojan can allow an attacker complete control over a computer. It can be used for stealing information or spying.








    ROOTKIT TROJAN

    The goal of a rootkit is to disguise or hide an object on your infected machine. The goal is to make a malicious program run longer on a device.










    SMS TROJAN

    This Trojan infects mobile devices and has the ability to send and receive text messages. Texting premium-rate numbers might increase your phone bill.









    TROJAN BANKER

    This Trojan is designed to target bank accounts. Its purpose is to steal account information for whatever you do online. This includes information about banking, credit cards, and bill payments.


    TROJAN IM

    Instant messaging is the objective of this Trojan. On IM systems, it takes usernames and passwords.
