    Trojan software infections can do a great deal of harm. Trojans, on the other hand, are still evolving.

    RAKHNI TROJAN

    Since 2013, this virus has been around. It can now infect PCs with malware or cryptojackers (which allow crooks to mine cryptocurrency using your device).

    “The growth in coin mining in the final months of 2017 was immense”

    ZeuS/Zbot

    Another oldie but goldie is this banking Trojan. The source code for ZeuS/Zbot was initially made public in 2011. It steals your credentials and maybe your account balance by using keystroke logging – recording your keystrokes while you log into your bank account, for example. Read the full case study on Zeus here.
