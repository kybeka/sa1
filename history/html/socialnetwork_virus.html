    The new frontier of Virus: Social Network

    Today viruses are created and spread their-self in a whole different way following a phenomenon that almost twenty years ago was unimaginable. During the evolution of the digital era, social networks can be seen as an extraordinary instrument to help people meet from all over the world and create a more globalised world. If it’s important to consider every positive aspect in a completely new way of connecting cultures and societies from different parts of the world is equally important to consider that these instruments can have side effects especially when the actions of a single smart hacker can affect the life of millions of persons.

    Fake Accounts

    The first and most obvious instrument that hackers use to compromise and steal data exactly like a malware or ransomware is impersonation. In fact profile cloning is one the most common hacking method used to create fake accounts that emulate a person’s account known by the target in order to making the target person more likely to share information or click on phishing links.

    This impersonation method is used either to emulate personal or brands account, in these cases the attackers emulate companies officials account in order to reach the target person by offering fake costumer support of fake help offers that contains phishing links or similar. Another malignant use of fake accounts is to inflate reactions, likes or clicks with the intent of encouraging social network’s to prioritise the content and manipulate the target account.
